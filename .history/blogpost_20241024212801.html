The Future of Artificial Intelligence: Trends to Watch in 2025

by Sarah Khan | October 12, 2024

Artificial intelligence (AI) has rapidly evolved over the past decade, transforming industries and reshaping the way we interact with technology. From healthcare to finance, AI's impact is undeniable, and its potential for future innovations seems limitless.

Machine learning, a subset of AI, has been at the forefront of recent breakthroughs. It enables systems to learn from data and improve over time without being explicitly programmed. This has opened the door to applications such as autonomous vehicles, personalized recommendations, and advanced natural language processing.
